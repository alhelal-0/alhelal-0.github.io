Publications

Dr. Hamdy's work focuses on the Computer Networks, Wireless Sensor Network (WSNs) Applications and Neural Network Applications.
His experimental work spans over the following areas of applications: Smart Mobile/stationary WSNs for Asyncronous Even Detection, e.g., Forest-Fire, Border Intrusion, Tsunami, Volcanic Activities, etc., Wireless Networks Security Protocols, All-Fiber-Optics Networks(AFOP) Admission Control, Smart Big-Data/Cloud-Computing Management.
